--- 高低差视觉 (ElevationVisionSettings) ---
   [原文] High Elevation Angle Range
   [译文] 高仰角角度范围
   [解释] The difference of angle from the bot's vision to the enemy to fully apply HighElevationVisionModifier. The modifier is smoothed out by the angle difference. So 1.2x at +60 degree, 1.1x at +30 degrees...and so on.
   [解释译文] Bot视线到敌人的角度差，达到此值时完全应用高仰角视觉修正。修正值随角度差平滑过渡。例如+60度时1.2倍，+30度时1.1倍...以此类推。

   [原文] High Elevation Vision Modifier
   [译文] 高仰角视觉修正值
   [解释] Bots will see players this much slower when the enemy's altitude is higher than the bot when the vision angle difference is equal or greater than HighElevationMaxAngle. Higher is slower speed, so 1.2 would result in bots taking 20% longer to spot an enemy
   [解释译文] 当敌人高于Bot且视角差达到高仰角角度范围时，Bot发现玩家的速度乘以此值。数值越高=越慢。例如1.2意味着Bot需要多花20%的时间来发现敌人。

   [原文] Low Elevation Angle Range
   [译文] 低俯角角度范围
   [解释] The difference of angle from the bot's vision to the enemy to fully apply LowElevationVisionModifier. The modifier is smoothed out by the angle difference. So 0.85x at -30 degree, 0.95x at -10 degrees...and so on.
   [解释译文] Bot视线到敌人的角度差，达到此值时完全应用低俯角视觉修正。修正值随角度差平滑过渡。

   [原文] Low Elevation Vision Modifier
   [译文] 低俯角视觉修正值
   [解释] Bots will see sprinting players this much slower when the enemy's altitude is lower than the bot when the vision angle difference is equal or greater than LowElevationMaxAngle. Higher is slower speed, so 0.85 would result in bots taking 15% shorter to spot an enemy
   [解释译文] 当敌人低于Bot且视角差达到低俯角角度范围时，Bot发现玩家的速度乘以此值。数值越低=越快。例如0.85意味着Bot发现敌人的时间缩短15%。

--- 移动视觉 (MovementVisibilitySettings) ---
   [原文] Movement Vision Modifier
   [译文] 移动视觉修正值
   [解释] Bots will see moving players this much faster, at any range. Higher is slower speed, so 0.66 would result in bots spotting an enemy who is moving 0.66x faster. So if they usually would take 10 seconds to spot someone, it would instead take around 6.6 seconds.
   [解释译文] Bot发现移动中的玩家速度乘以此值，适用于任意距离。数值越低=辨识越快。如0.66意味着原来10秒的发现时间缩短为约6.6秒。

--- 视觉距离 (VisionDistanceSettings) ---
   [原文] Movement Vision Distance Modifier
   [译文] 移动视觉距离修正值
   [解释] Bots will see moving players this much further. Higher is further distance, so 1.75 would result in bots seeing enemies 1.75x further at max player speed. Scales with player velocity.
   [解释译文] Bot发现移动中玩家的距离乘以此值。数值越高=距离越远。例如1.75意味着以最大速度移动的玩家被Bot看到的距离为1.75倍。随玩家速度缩放。

--- 周边视野 (PeripheralVisionSettings) ---
   [原文] 字段无Name属性，但有类级别Description
   [解释] Adds additional vision speed reduction to targets in a bot's peripheral vision. Scales with the angle from their look direction.
   [解释译文] 对Bot周边视野内的目标施加额外的视觉速度减益，随视线角度缩放。

--- 第三方视野 (ThirdPartySettings) ---
   [解释] When an enemy is a certain angle away from their active enemies last known position, this will reduce their vision speed of that target up to the maximum set amount.
   [解释译文] 当敌人位于Bot当前活跃目标的最后已知位置一定角度外时，对该目标的视觉速度最多降低到设定的最大值。

--- 身体部位可见度 (PartsVisibilitySettings) ---
   [解释] Scales vision speed based on the number of body parts that are within line of sight to their enemy. Only applies to Non-AI targets.
   [解释译文] 根据Bot视线内敌人可见的身体部位数量缩放视觉速度。仅对非AI目标(玩家)生效。

--- 姿态可见度 (PoseVisibilitySettings) ---
   [解释] Scales vision speed based on the pose of their enemy. Only applies to Non-AI targets.
   [解释译文] 根据敌人的姿态(站立/蹲下/趴下)缩放视觉速度。仅对非AI目标(玩家)生效。
